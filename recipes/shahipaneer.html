Shahi Paneer


About


Shahi Paneer or Paneer Rajwadi or Chhena Rajwadi or Raajsi Chhena is a preparation of chhena or paneer, native to the Indian subcontinent, consisting of a thick gravy of cream, tomatoes and Indian spices.This dish is prepared by emulsifying tomatoes, onions, ground cashews, clarified butter and cream into a curry, with the addition of chhena/paneer cubes and a variety of spices.

Ingredients


  • 500 grams paneer
  • 2 onion
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 3 green chilli
  • 3 green cardamom
  • 1/2 cup yoghurt(curd)
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli
  • 6 tablespoon ghee
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 cup milk
  • 11/2 cup tomato puree
  • salt as required
  • water as required
  • 1/2 cup cashews
  • 1/2 cup almonds

    • For Garnishing

  • 1 handful coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream

Steps


  1. Chop all the veggies and make almond-cashew paste
    To prepare this creamy shahi paneer recipe, chop the onion, green chillies, tomatoes along with ginger and coriander leaves seperately.Now in a bowl, add the curd and whisk it well. If you are in a hurry, you can also use tomato puree instead of chopped tomatoes. To make this recipe more delicious, you can dry roast the spices seperately and then add them to the gravy. This will make your recipe more aromatic. Now, grind cashews and almonds seperately using a little water and make cashew and almond paste. Keep them aside until required.
  2. Prepare onion-tomato gravy using curd
    Next, put a pan on medium flame and heat 3 tablespoon of ghee in it. Add the chopped onions, ginger, green chillies along with green cardamoms for 4-5 minutes. Add the tomato puree and cover the pan with lid. Cook for about 8-10 minutes. Then, add the beaten curd, cook for another 5 minutes and pour a cup of water into the pan. Cook for another 2 minutes. When the gravy is cooked, let it cool down at room temperature. When it's cool enough, add it to a mixer jar and blend till smooth and keep aside
  3. Add paneer cubes in the gravy and cook for 3-5 minutes
    Now, heat the remaining ghee in another pan and add the ground gravy in it along with red chilli powder, a garam masala powder, cashew and almond paste along with salt. Bring it to a boil or till gravy thickens. Then add the paneer cubes and milk. Cook for another 3-5 minutes. While the gravy is being cooked, cut the paneer into cubes and keep it aside till required. Take two cubes and grate them for garnishing.
  4. Garnish and serve
    Garnish this delicious North Indian paneer recipe with chopped corriander leaves and grated paneer. If you like it creamier, you can add some fresh cream. This will not only make your dish look appetisig but at the same time, it will add to the taste. Serve hot with roomali roti or naan and enjoy!
