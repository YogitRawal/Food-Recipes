Butter Paneer


About


Butter Paneer (also called paneer makhani) is an Indian dish of paneer, originating in New Delhi, in which the gravy is prepared usually with butter(makhan), tomatoes and cashews. Spices such as red chilli powder and garam masala are also used to prepare this gravy. Paneer butter masala is one of the top five foods ordered in India.

Ingredients


  • 250 grams paneer
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 11/2 tablespoon cashew nut paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 3 tablespoon fresh cream
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • 2 tablespoon butter
  • salt as required
  • 1 teaspoon kasoori methi powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala powder

Steps


  1. Prepare the tomato-cashew nut gravy
    To prepare this easy paneer recipe, tale a pan and melt ghee in it. Once the ghee is melted, add ginger garlic paste and saute these ingredients for half a minute. Then add tomato puree to it. Stir well and then add cashew nut paste along with red chilli powder and salt. Mix well and cook for 2 minutes.
  2. Cook paneer in this gravy for 2 minutes
    Now, add honey, kasoori methi powder, butter in the garvy and mix well. Cook for 2-3 minutes and then add paneer pieces to it. Stir gently and coat the gravy well over the paneer pieces.
  3. Garnish with fresh cream and serve
    Add fresh cream (keep 1 tablespoon aside) in the gravy along with 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala. Cook for 2-3 minutes. Your butter paneer masala is ready! Garnish with remaining fresh cream and chaat masala. Serve butter paneer hot with paratha, rotis or rice!
